Veg Manchurian Recipe

About Food:

Manchurian is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made with deep-fried vegetable or meatballs coated in a
savory, tangy sauce. The dish is known for its unique blend of Chinese cooking techniques and
Indian spices, creating a flavorful and aromatic dish. It is often served as an appetizer or main
course and is loved for its bold flavors and crunchy texture.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped capsicum
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix the chopped vegetables, cornflour, all-purpose flour, soy sauce, tomato ketchup, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Shape the mixture into small balls and deep fry them until golden brown. Drain and set aside.
