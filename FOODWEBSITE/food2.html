Spaghetti Carbonara

About Food:

Paneer Kathi Roll is a popular street food in India, consisting of spiced paneer wrapped in a paratha or roti.
The paneer is marinated in a blend of spices and yogurt, then cooked until tender and flavorful.
It is typically served with green chutney and onions, making it a delicious and satisfying meal or snack.

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cut into thin strips
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder,1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 2-3 tablespoons tomato ketchup or chutney
  • 2-3 large chapatis or parathas
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan. Add the paneer strips and sauté until lightly browned. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add the remaining oil. Add the sliced onions and bell peppers. Sauté until they are soft and slightly caramelized.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the sautéed paneer back to the pan. Stir in the tomato ketchup or chutney. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Warm the chapatis or parathas on a griddle or tawa.
  7. Place a portion of the paneer mixture on each chapati or paratha. Roll them up tightly.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with chopped coriander leaves and lemon wedges on the side.
