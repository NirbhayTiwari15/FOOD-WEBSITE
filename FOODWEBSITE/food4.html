Shahi Paneer

About Food:

Shahi Paneer is a popular North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a
creamy and flavorful tomato-based gravy. The gravy is enriched with cream, cashews, and a blend of
aromatic spices, giving the dish its rich and indulgent taste. It is often served with rice or
Indian breads like naan or roti. Shahi Paneer is a favorite in vegetarian Indian cuisine, loved
for its royal taste and creamy texture.

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts, soaked
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they are translucent.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the ghee separates from the masala.
  5. Meanwhile, blend the soaked cashew nuts with a little water to make a smooth paste.
  6. Add the cashew paste to the masala and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  8. Add the paneer cubes and mix gently to coat them with the masala.
  9. Add water as needed to achieve the desired consistency of the gravy.
  10. Cover and simmer for 5-10 minutes on low heat.
  11. Stir in the fresh cream and garam masala. Cook for another minute.
  12. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  13. Serve hot with naan or rice.
