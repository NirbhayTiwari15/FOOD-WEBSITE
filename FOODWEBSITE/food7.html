Shahi Chicken Korma

About Food:

Shahi Chicken Korma is a regal Mughlai dish known for its rich and creamy texture. Tender
chicken is marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices, then cooked in a luxurious sauce made
with cream, almonds, and aromatic spices like cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon. This dish is
often garnished with fried onions and nuts, adding to its royal appeal. Shahi Chicken Korma is
a delightful indulgence, perfect for special occasions or when you want to treat yourself to a
taste of royalty.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts, soaked and ground to a paste
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, cumin powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they are translucent.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook until it is browned.
  4. Add the cashew nut paste and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add enough water to cover the chicken and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Stir in the cream and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
