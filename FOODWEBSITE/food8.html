Dal Makhni Recipe

About Food:

Dal Makhni is a popular North Indian dish made from black lentils and kidney beans cooked with
butter and cream. It is seasoned with aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala,
giving it a rich and creamy texture. Dal Makhni is often served with rice or naan bread, and is a
comfort food enjoyed by many.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup black lentils (whole urad dal)
  • 1/4 cup kidney beans (rajma)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak the black lentils and kidney beans in water for at least 4 hours or overnight. Drain and rinse.
  2. Pressure cook the lentils and kidney beans with enough water and a little salt until they are soft and cooked.
  3. Heat butter in a pan. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they are translucent.
  4. Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  5. Add the tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook until the oil separates.
  6. Add the cooked lentils and kidney beans along with some water. Simmer for about 20-30 minutes.
  7. Stir in the cream and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
